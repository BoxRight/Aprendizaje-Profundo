Limitaciones de Radar
• Factores ambientales como la interferencia de dispositivos
electrónicos y materiales de metal pueden afectar la señal
• Cambios en los muebles, o introducción de objetos grandes
puede requerir recalibración
• La resolución de los radares depende mucho de la calidad del
hardware

Juegos de datos de Radar
Nombre
mmFall Dataset

Descripción
URL
This dataset is collected with millimeter-wave (mmWave) radar and https://github.com/radar-lab/mmfall
includes point cloud data for various activities and simulated falls.
Features point clouds, body centroid positions, and is available for
research use. The collection protocol focuses on privacy and high
sensitivity to motion, with semi-supervised labeling based on
inference from normal daily activity

PeerJ Multimodal Dataset

Contains synchronized recordings from multiple sensors, including https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC119704
60–64 GHz radar, 8×8 LIDAR, FIR thermal camera, and a mobile 14/
phone’s 3D accelerometer. Its design is privacy-conscious (no
RGB cameras) and supports sensor fusion research. The dataset
documents ten participants and ten fall types, along with multiple
https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/YJGDV
daily activities

PARrad Dataset

Focuses on healthcare support via activity recognition with radar,
including critical events like falls, though specific download
instructions and meta-data should be verified by contacting the
project directly

Radar Human Activity Datasets Datasets such as the one in Nature (2023) contain radar data for
six types of daily human activities, including falling, collected for
activity recognition and fall detection
